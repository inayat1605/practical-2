(3 food items you must try in
chandigarh)

1. White Sauce Pasta/Penne Pesto

(Ovenfresh - sector 7 / sector 35 / Elante mall)

If you want a perfect creamy and rich white sauce pasta, perfectly balanced with flavors of garlic and herbs, then Ovenfresh is the best choice. It's a comforting dish, popular for its smooth texture and generous portions, making it a favorite among pasta lovers in the city.

2. Butter Garlic Momos

(Social - sector 7 / Elante mall)

Butter garlic momos are a delightful twist on the classic dumpling, with soft, juicy fillings enveloped in a golden, buttery crust. Each bite is a burst of rich, garlicky goodness, making it an irresistible treat for your taste buds. These momos offer a perfect harmony of warmth and flavor, making them a must-try for any food lover.

3.Paradise Pie

(Chili's Grill & Bar - sector 26 / Elante mall)

Chocolate chip, walnut and coconut layered brownie bar. Topped with walnuts and drizzeled with hot fudge and caramel , served with ice-cream. A mouth watering and tempting dessert, almost impossible to resist, is what this brownie with ice cream holds in it.
